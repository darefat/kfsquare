Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly can you start a project?

We can typically start new projects within 1-2 weeks after contract signing. For urgent projects, we offer expedited onboarding within 3-5 business days.

Do you work with small businesses?

Absolutely! As a certified small business ourselves, we specialize in helping other small and medium businesses leverage data analytics. We offer flexible pricing and scaled solutions.

What industries do you serve?

We serve 40+ industries including healthcare, finance, retail, manufacturing, government, and technology. Our team has experience across diverse sectors.

Do you provide ongoing support?

Yes, we offer comprehensive support packages including 24/7 monitoring, regular updates, training, and dedicated account management.

Can you work with our existing team?

Definitely! We excel at augmenting existing teams and can work in various engagement models: fully managed, collaborative, or consultative.

What's your typical project timeline?

Project timelines vary from 2-4 weeks for simple analytics dashboards to 6-12 months for complex AI implementations. We'll provide a detailed timeline during consultation.
