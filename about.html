Our Story

From service to
software.

Founded by veterans who understood firsthand that the difference between a good decision and a bad one often comes down to the quality of the information behind it.

KFSQUARE was founded on a straightforward premise: organizations deserve data systems that actually work. Not demos. Not decks. Working software and pipelines that produce results their teams can act on.

We started in government — where the requirements are strict, the stakes are high, and the timelines are real. That discipline shaped how we deliver for every client since, commercial or federal.

Today our team spans software engineers, data scientists, cloud architects, and automation specialists. We are small by design. Every engagement gets senior attention, not junior hand-offs.
